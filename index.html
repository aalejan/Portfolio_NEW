About Me

Hello! I'm a software developer whose love for coding sparked from exploring online courses on Udemy. Initially driven by curiosity, I quickly found myself enthralled by the process of building solutions and solving problems with code. My passion eventually led me to a rigorous bootcamp, where I immersed myself in the fascinating world of web development.

Over the years, I've been able to work on a variety of intriguing projects. While I've had notable success with improving user experiences on large-scale surveys (even enhancing interfaces for over 30,000 mental health facilities), my portfolio extends well beyond that. I've developed dynamic visualization applications, integrated custom JavaScript functionalities, and played a key role in the development of diverse features for different projects.

Working in agile Scrum teams has allowed me to understand the significance of organization and prioritization in software development. I've found it rewarding to prioritize user stories and estimate task efforts to ensure efficient project management. I'm also a strong advocate for clean code and regularly refine codebases for improved readability. I enjoy getting hands-on with a wide variety of tools in software development, and while I'm proficient in specific tools like Voxco, my true passion lies in constantly learning and applying new technologies.
